About Guardian Care

Guardian Care is designed for people who travel abroad for a long time (6 to 12 months). While they are away, our system makes sure that important things like car maintenance, pet care, house cleaning, and plant watering are handled by trusted local service providers.

Users can log in, add their details, and book the services they need. They can also set their travel start and end dates to ensure everything runs smoothly while they are away. Service providers can view their assigned tasks and update the status once they complete them, while administrators oversee and manage all services.

Our mission is simple — to give you peace of mind during your time abroad by making sure your home and personal needs are handled with care and reliability.
